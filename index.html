Learn More

BLASTBioinformatics is an initiative intented to engage highschool students into the field of bioinformatics. We have developed a series of workshops to introduce various fundamental concepts in bioinformatics to provide an overview of the field. In general, bioinformatics can be considered a scientific discipline focused on solving complex biological problems by utilizing computational capabilities.

Genomics

Unlocking mysteries within our DNA sequences

Computation

Using computational advancements to solve biological problems

Scientific Inquiry

Asking and solving complex biological problems.

Workshops

Learn more about the different workshop sessions

  • January 27, 2022

    Week 1 - Bioinformatic Databases

    This session will focus on the learning how to use and navigate bioinformatic databases, as well as explore the types of genomic data that can be used for bioinformatic analyses.

  • February 3, 2022

    Week 2 - Phylogenetics

    In this session, participants will learn about what phylogenetics is, and how to use tools to conduct phylogenetic analyses.

  • February 10, 2022

    Week 3 - Intro to R programming and Sequence Logos

    This session will serve as an introduction on how to write basic scripts in R to generate bioinformatic visualizations.

  • February 17, 2022

    Week 4 - Protein Modelling

    This session will focus using proteonomic data to develop 3D protein models.

  • February 24, 2022

    Week 5 - Presentations and Q&A Panel

    In our final session, participants will put together a presentation to talk about their findings throughout the workshop. Following presentations, a Q&A panel will be held with individuals conduct bioinformatic research.
